Application: Solution Overview

01

Solution Name

AG999 Climate Risk Engine — a physics-informed, nonlinear climate crisis prediction platform for circular economy systems.

02

Problem Addressed

Climate-related disruptions are generating cascading failures across waste management, water systems, logistics, and critical circular infrastructure. Organizations operating in climate-vulnerable regions — particularly across Indonesia's islands — lack integrated predictive tools capable of anticipating these failures before they escalate into humanitarian crises. Existing solutions are reactive, siloed, and unable to model nonlinear interactions between climate stressors and circular system performance.

03

Proposed Solution

AG999 is a real-time climate crisis signal engine powered by two proprietary mathematical layers: Formula 06A — the operational engine computing crisis probability from climate and circular economy variables — and Formula 04A — the physical foundation derived from nonlinear electrodynamics field theory. Together, they produce a continuous, probabilistic risk signal with early warning lead times of 6 to 18 months, enabling proactive adaptation by municipalities, utilities, and supply chain operators.

04

How It Works in Practice

  • Operators input real-time normalized circular economy indicators (waste diversion, water recycling efficiency, infrastructure resilience, climate stress)
  • Engine computes D²_t stress tensor integrating cross-variable correlations and memory effects
  • Operator O(D²) filters nonlinear transitions: INACTIVE → ATTENUATING → SUPPRESSING → TOTAL BLOCKING
  • Crisis signal output (0–1) with actionable recommendations and lead time estimate
  • Dashboard accessible via web, PWA/offline mode, 7 languages including Bahasa Indonesia

05

Technological Innovation

  • Formula 04A: derived from NLED-EFT (Non-Linear Electrodynamics Effective Field Theory). Ghost-free, UV-finite propagator. Integrates quantum state memory effects (Bloch sphere formalism) with classical operational data
  • Formula 06A: operationalizes 04A into a deployable sigmoid-based probability engine with short-term (φ_t, 6-month) and long-term (σ_t, 24-month) exponential memory fields and a latent fragility state z_t
  • SHA-256 hash registered March 2026. No prior art identified. No arXiv, no patent — full IP retained by founder
  • Unique cross-term covariance structure (w_IW, w_WC) captures cascading failure dynamics absent from all competing models
